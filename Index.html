Branding Idea

What does the company do, or offer?

Certified Cell is an online marketplace allows people to buy or sell a certified and local quality used iPhone. The ultimate goal of Certified Cell is to provide a safe pickup at a trusted retail location instead of meeting with random strangers in parking lots, a secure payment so that buyers could buy any iPhone on the website with a credit card online instead of leaving stack of cash at home, and an affordable price since Certified Cell combines the quality of a certified pre-owned iPhone & the value of buying from a local seller.All devices listed on Certified Cell website have been certified by a 21-point inspection by a trusted technician.

Who is the company to potential customers?(as a person)

Anyone who wants to buy or sell a phone with safe pickup, secure payment and affordable price.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Certied Cell provides safety security, and peace-of-mind throughout the process of buying or selling a phone. Buyers on Certified Cell use a quick and easy online platform to find the perfect phone and sellers experience a streamlined cash-less way to sell their quality pre-owned phones using a trusted local retail location.
