Define the basic branding ideas



What does the company do, or offer?



Cerified Cell is a company that buys and sells secondhand smartphones (iPhone specifically). They offer a rather reasonable price with certified quality iPhones comparing to other sellers. Meanwhile, they also provide a much safer experience as customers can avoid meeting a stranger at a unfimiliar location by buying it at a trusted retail physical store. That being said, customers can also choose to go onto Certified Cell website, select their own carrier (ex: Verizon, AT&T,T-Mobile), phone color, capacity, condition of the phone, and which store to pick up.



Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)



People who have old iPhones; people who want to get a quality iPhone with low budgets; people who want a safer experience (transaction safety/personal safety); people who like Apple products.



Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service



Certified Cell is known as a safe online platform, it attracts people with its low price, but guaranteed quality secondhand iPhones (with customized color/capacity/carrier). As mentioned above, the biggest advantage of purchasing iPhone products through Certified Cell is that it provides a much safer transaction as customers can avoid meeting a stranger at a random location by buying it at a trusted retail physical store.
